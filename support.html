Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a link?

Tap the + Add button on the home screen, paste a URL, give it a title, and tap Save. You can also share directly from Safari using the Share button.

What is a Zone?

A Zone is a named location (like "Home", "Office", or "Tokyo Trip"). When you save a link, you assign it to a Zone. Loci surfaces those saves when you're nearby.

Why isn't my location being detected?

Make sure Location permission is set to "Always" in iPhone Settings → Loci → Location. Background detection requires the "Always" permission.

I purchased Pro but it's not active.

Go to Profile → Restore Purchases to restore your subscription. If that doesn't work, email us and we'll help.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Loci and tap Cancel Subscription. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your saves, notes, and photos are stored privately in your account. We never share your data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
