Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Loci ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data we collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide the service: your email address and name when you create an account, the links and notes you choose to save, zone names and locations you set, and basic usage data to improve the app.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide Loci's features — storing your saves, powering zone-based surfacing, and syncing across your devices. We do not sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising.

Location data

Loci uses your device location to detect when you enter a zone and surface relevant saves. Location data is processed on-device and is not stored on our servers beyond the coordinates you explicitly set for your zones.

Third-party services

Loci uses Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage, and RevenueCat for in-app purchase management. Both services have their own privacy policies. Sign-in via Google or Apple is subject to their respective privacy policies.

Data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile tab inside the app. Deletion is permanent and immediate.
