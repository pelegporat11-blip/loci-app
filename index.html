Features

Everything you save,
right where you need it.

Stop hunting through bookmarks. Loci brings your saved links to the surface the moment you're in the right place.

📍

Location-aware saves

Attach a location to any link. When you enter that zone, Loci surfaces it automatically — no searching required.

🗺️

Custom zones

Create named zones for the places that matter — Kitchen, Office, Gym, Guitar Corner. Group your saves by where they belong.

🔗

Save anything

Articles, recipes, videos, products — Loci auto-detects the content type and organises it for you.

📷

Photo attachments

Attach a photo to any save. Snap the menu, the shelf, the label — and find it exactly when you're back.

🌐

9 languages

Full RTL support. Loci speaks English, Hebrew, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Japanese, Arabic and Chinese.

⚡

Lightning fast

Built natively for iOS. No web wrapper, no lag. Your saves open instantly from anywhere in the app.

How it works

Three steps. Then forget about it.

1

Save a link

Paste any URL into Loci. Give it a title, add notes if you want, and pick which zone it belongs to.

2

Set a zone

Create a zone for your current location — your kitchen, your favourite coffee shop, your guitar corner.

3

Arrive and open

When you enter that zone, Loci notifies you. Tap to open your saves — right place, right time.
