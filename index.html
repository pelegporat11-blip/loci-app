How it works

Three steps to smarter saves

Loci connects your saved content to the real world, so the right thing appears at the right time.

1

Save a link

Save any link, video, recipe, or article. Loci auto-fetches the title so you don't have to type anything.

2

Pin it to a Zone

Tag your save to a Zone — Kitchen, Gym, Coffee Shop, Airport. Anywhere that has meaning to you.

3

Arrive and discover

When you enter a Zone, Loci surfaces everything you saved there. No searching, no forgetting.
